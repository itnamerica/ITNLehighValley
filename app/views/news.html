Independent Transportation Network Lehigh Valley (ITNLehighValley) is currently seeking energetic, motivated professionals who wish to enhance the lives of seniors through service on the ITNLehighValley Board of Directors. We are looking for people with a personal and professional network who can share their passion for and advance our mission of providing 24/7 transportation to seniors and visually impaired adults when and where they want it. For more information, please email info@itnlehighvalley.org or call 610-419-1645.
