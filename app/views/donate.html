Got an old vehicle sitting silent? Thinking about limiting your own or a loved one’s driving? Consider donating your vehicle to ITNLehighValley! Your old car can leave a legacy of future rides even after it’s gone.

Did you know that according to the Huffington Post, annual vehicle usage costs for a Pennsylvania driver in 2016 amounted to $3,249. At an average cost of $11 per ride that comes to almost 300 rides a year with ITNLehighValley….almost one ride/day!

When you are ready to donate your vehicle, call Donate For Charity toll-free at (866) 392-4483 or donate on-line by clicking on Donate your Vehicle.
